The Problem

35 million pet households.
Zero digital infrastructure.

A broken, fragmented system that fails pet owners, vets, municipalities — and the animals themselves.

No National Registry
KCI records are paper-based pedigree-only. BBMP has issued only ~2,100 licences ever in Bengaluru. BMC ward records are non-digital. Local pilots (WSD SoBo, BBMP Happy Pet) remain isolated and unscalable. Pet registration compliance is below 5% nationally.
<5% compliance nationally
No Real-Time Health Layer
No continuous biometric monitoring exists anywhere in India. Obesity, cardiac events, and early-stage illness go undetected until crisis. Vets see pets only at clinic visits — with no health data between appointments. India's pet insurance market is USD 662M but insured pets are a tiny fraction of the 100M population.
0 AI health monitoring platforms
No Digital Identity
Lost pets have no scannable, searchable ID. Reunification is entirely luck-based. No national matching system exists. India's 33.6M dogs are 87.4% of the pet population — and virtually none have a permanent digital identity.
0 digital ID platforms in India
Fragmented Ecosystem
Food, grooming, sitters, walkers, toys, vet care, boarding — all completely siloed. India imported 21,133 pet food shipments in FY24 alone, yet distribution is through unorganized local retailers with no unified tracking. No single platform connects a pet owner's entire world.
0 end-to-end pet platforms
No End-of-Life Framework
When a pet passes, owners navigate cremation, record closure, and licence cancellation entirely alone. Municipal records are not updated. Pets continue to appear in active databases for years post-death. An underserved, emotionally significant gap with no existing solution.
100% manual, unmanaged today
Zero Adoption Infrastructure
India's shelters are paper-based with zero outcome data. No digital matching between adopters and rescuable pets. 70% of Indian pet owners are first-timers (2024) — yet there's no onboarding infrastructure to guide responsible ownership. The adoption pipeline from shelter to forever home is completely digital-dark.
